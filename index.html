What We Do

Guard Guys’ OnSITE Drug Testing and Consulting is a local, VA certified, Veteran Owned Small Business that hosts a broad spectrum of drug/alcohol testing, along with additional services including DOT physicals, DNA, TB and COVID-19 screenings.

  • Drug/Alcohol - our tests will detect drug and alcohol usage levels in all mediums including urine, saliva, breath, blood, and hair. Instant results are available with certain types of screenings. In addition to providing testing for individual purposes such as family and court actions, we provide orginzations with pre-employment, random, post-accident and for reasonable suspicion situations
  • DOT Physicals - we have a certified doctor on staff that provides exams for DOT medical cards
  • DNA - legal, immigration, paternity and pre-natal gender identification
  • TB - QuantiFERON Gold
  • COVID-19 - rapid antigen, antibody and PCR

In addition to providing non-regulated tests/screenings for our customers, OnSITE is also certified by the Department of Transportation to conduct federally regulated drug and alcohol testing for all of their agencies including the FMCSA, FAA, FRA etc. We also provide Consortium/Third Party Administrator services including compliance management, training and random DOT consortium pools.
